# Data and Code Availability

**Code.** The analysis code is archived in this repository and on Zenodo (software DOI to be
inserted on release) and covers the full processing chain from Sentinel-2 export through
feature construction, selection, Random Forest classification, temporal filtering, and
validation. See `README.md` for the file-to-method mapping. Released under the MIT License.

**Maps.** The annual classified *Pinus mugo* maps (2017–2025, 10 m, EPSG:25832) and the
permanent presence/absence layer are archived on Zenodo:
https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.19691239

**Training and validation data.** The reference and validation point datasets (coordinates
and presence/absence labels) generated for this study are available [in the Zenodo archive /
on reasonable request — choose one]. These points were produced by manual interpretation of
Bavarian Digital Orthophotos (DOP20, 2024) provided by the Bavarian Agency for Digitisation,
High-Speed Internet and Surveying (LDBV); the orthophotos themselves are distributed by LDBV
under their own terms and are not redistributed here.
